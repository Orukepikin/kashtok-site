Built for Real Life

Designed for the
Nigerian money reality.

We didn't copy a Silicon Valley fintech. We built KashTok from scratch around how Nigerians actually live, earn and spend.

🌙

Late-night bill panic?

AEDC disconnects at 11pm on a Friday? No problem. KashTok works 24/7. Vend token, restore power — in under 30 seconds from your bed.

📅

Salary delay survival mode

Set a "Survival Fund" vault before month-end. KashTok auto-saves a fixed amount every pay day so you always have runway when salary delays.

🖤

Track your black tax

Family responsibilities are real. KashTok lets you tag transfers as "family" so you can see exactly how much you support others — and plan around it.

📶

Built for patchy internet

KashTok is engineered for low-bandwidth connections. Fast even on 3G. Transactions queue and complete when connection restores. No failed payments.

💵

No more dollar card stress

Your KashTok virtual card works for Netflix, Spotify, Canva, AWS — all the subscriptions that used to require begging a friend with a dollar card.

👨‍👩‍👧

Send to anyone in Nigeria

Transfer to all 20+ Nigerian banks instantly. Search by phone number or name — no account number memorization required. Just tap and send.
