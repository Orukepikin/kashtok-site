Our Story

Built by Nigerians.
Built for Nigerians.

KashTok was born from a simple frustration — why is managing money in Nigeria so unnecessarily complicated and expensive?

The problem we're solving

The average Nigerian touches at least four different apps to manage their money every day — one for salary, another for bills, another for savings, another for airtime. Screenshots are shared as proof of payment. Bank apps crash during Ember months. Hidden fees appear on statements nobody reads.

We built KashTok to collapse all of that into one calm, fast, smart experience — designed specifically around how Nigerians actually live with money.

🎯

Our Mission

To make financial clarity and control available to every Nigerian — whether they earn ₦50,000 or ₦5,000,000 a month.

👁️

Our Vision

A Nigeria where your money works as hard as you do — growing quietly in the background while you focus on life.
