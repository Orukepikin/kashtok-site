Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

KashTok takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

What we collect

We collect your email address when you join the waitlist. When you create a full account, we collect identity verification data (BVN, selfie), contact information, and transaction data necessary to provide financial services.

How we use your data

We use your data to operate the KashTok platform, verify your identity, process transactions, prevent fraud, and communicate important updates. We never sell your personal data to third parties.
